The Case

What Size Do You Need?

There are three basic sizes of cases to choose from: full atx, mid atx, and micro atx. Mid atx is the most popular choice, but if your want a super powerful gaming computer you may want full atx, and if you want a more portable PC with less power, micro atx is a great choice.

When you pick your case, make sure that the motherboard and GPU will fit. Also, make sure that it has the right number of ports for the hard drives, ssds, and any other drives that you want to put in it.

What Parts Come With the Case?

Some cases come with other parts. It's common for cases to come with a power supply and fans. Be sure to check what if it comest with these parts, because if it doesn't, you'll need to purchase them seperatly.
