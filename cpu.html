The CPU

Which Brand Should You Get?

The CPU is required in order for your computer run. When choosing a CPU, you can buy one of two brands. there's Intel and AMD. Some people fight over which one is better, so it really just depends on which one you think is best. I prefer Intel because from my experience they're easier to overclock, and they don't get as hot as AMD chips. Some people say that AMD chips are better for gamming though, but I think that's just a rumor.

What Speed Do You Need?

Choosing a CPU use to be fairly simple. Faster clock speed that the CPU had, the better it was. Nowadays we have things like multicore CPUs, turbo speed, and other tricks that manufactures use to make CPUs faster. CPUs are important for things like loading times, file compression, and other logic based things. If your a video or photo editor, or you just want a really fast computer, you'll want a higher end CPU.
