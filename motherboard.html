The Motherboard

Which One Do You Need?

In order to find out which motherboard you need, you have to know what the rest of the computers components will be. One of the most important things to consider, is that Intel and AMD CPUs need different types of motherboards. When you look at the motherboard specs, you'll find which type of CPU it's compatable with. You also need to make sure that the RAM ports will support the RAM speed that your using, this is measured in mhz. Another thing to think about is how many GPUs you want on the motherboard, in this case you'll have to make sure that the motherboard supports multi-gpus for the specific brand of GPU that you're using.
