The RAM

What Brand Should you Get?

There are alot of brands of RAM to choose from. Different manufactures are better than others depending on the time of year it is. In my personal experience the best way to find good RAM is by reading reviews, and going with the ones that have better reviews.

How Many Gigabytes Do you Really Need?

The price of RAM has come down a lot since computers started to become popular. It use to be, that when you upgraded the amount of RAM in you would notice a performance boost. Things have changed however. Usually about 4 to 8 gigabites of RAM will give you plenty of performance, at a low cost. Some people have as much as 32 gigabites of RAM in their system which, in most cases, is overkill. The only reason that you might want that much RAM is if you multitask like crazy. If you're editing a video, and a few hundred pictures all at the same time, plus you have thiry tabs open in your web browser than you may benifit 32 gigabites of RAM.
